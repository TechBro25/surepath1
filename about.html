About SurePath School Shopping

Who We Are

SurePath School Shopping is Nigeria’s #1 destination for affordable, reliable, and modern school supplies. From textbooks to lunch boxes, uniforms to gadgets — we make shopping for students easier and smarter. We’re not just a store; we’re a movement to empower learners through convenience and technology.

Our mission is to simplify academic preparation with quality products, doorstep delivery, and unmatched customer support. Whether you're a student, teacher, or parent, SurePath is your ultimate school shopping partner.
